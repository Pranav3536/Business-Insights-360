─────────────────────────────
📌 PROBLEM STATEMENT
─────────────────────────────

Modern organizations generate vast amounts of data across Finance, Sales, Marketing, and Supply Chain. 
This data is often fragmented across systems, making it challenging 
to obtain a unified view of business performance.Without proper analytics, decision-makers 
struggle to track key metrics, identify growth opportunities, and detect 
operational inefficiencies, which can impact profitability 
and strategic planning.

─────────────────────────────
🔍 Key Challenges
─────────────────────────────

1️⃣ No unified view of overall business performance  
2️⃣ Difficulty tracking key metrics (Net Sales, Profit, GM %)  
3️⃣ Limited visibility into customer & product trends  
4️⃣ Forecast accuracy and operational inefficiencies  
5️⃣ Identifying high & low performing segments  

─────────────────────────────
💡 Solution
─────────────────────────────

Build an interactive BI dashboard using SQL and Power BI 
to consolidate data, visualize KPIs, and provide actionable 
insights for informed business decisions.
