─────────────────────────────
📌 PROBLEM STATEMENT
─────────────────────────────

Businesses generate large volumes of data from Finance, Sales, 
Marketing, and Supply Chain. This data is often scattered, 
making it hard to extract meaningful insights.

─────────────────────────────
🔍 Key Challenges
─────────────────────────────

1️⃣ No unified view of overall business performance  
2️⃣ Difficulty tracking key metrics (Net Sales, Profit, GM %)  
3️⃣ Limited visibility into customer & product trends  
4️⃣ Forecast accuracy and operational inefficiencies  
5️⃣ Identifying high & low performing segments  

─────────────────────────────
💡 Solution
─────────────────────────────

Build an interactive BI dashboard using SQL and Power BI 
to convert raw data into actionable business insights.
