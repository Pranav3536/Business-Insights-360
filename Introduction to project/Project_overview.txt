--> PROJECT TITLE
Business Insights 360 – End-to-End Business Analytics Dashboard

--> DOMAIN
Business Intelligence | Data Analytics | Sales & Financial Analytics

--> TOOLS & TECHNOLOGIES
SQL (Data Extraction & Transformation)
Power BI (Data Modeling, DAX, Interactive Dashboards)
Data Visualization & Business Intelligence Techniques

--> PROJECT SUMMARY
1. This project delivers an end-to-end business analytics solution to monitor and evaluate organizational performance across multiple departments.
2. The dashboard analyzes key metrics such as Net Sales, Gross Margin, Net Profit, customer performance, and product profitability.
3. It provides insights into financial performance, sales trends, marketing effectiveness, and supply chain forecasting.
4. The project simulates a real-world business intelligence environment where decision-makers rely on data-driven insights to improve operational efficiency and strategic planning.
